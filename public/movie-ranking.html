The Best Movies According to Akarsh

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

Movie with comedy, struggle, drama and a good climax with a flavour of suspense making it a must-watch.

12th Fail

Emotional and well-directed. Showcasing the undefined struggle by an UPSC Aspirant.

Andhadhund

Packed with mind-bending its twist and turns. Well-written by Sriram Raghavan.
